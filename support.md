## Frequently asked questions

### Is Color Bar really free?

Yes. The whole game is free with **no ads and no tracking** — nothing is paywalled. There's an optional "tip jar" (the heart on the home screen) if you'd like to support development, but it's entirely optional and unlocks nothing.

### I tipped — did I unlock anything? What if it didn't go through?

Tips are a small thank-you, not a purchase of content, so they unlock nothing — the game is already fully free. Apple processes every tip through the App Store; we never see your name, card, or payment details. If a tip didn't complete, Apple won't have charged you, and you can simply try again or skip it.

### What are the different ways to play?

- **New Game** — the endless campaign; the palette grows as you progress (blue, then white for tinting, then black for shading, then pre-mixed composite tubes you can pour or break back down).
- **Daily Challenge** — one shared puzzle a day for everyone.
- **Create a Level** — design and share your own puzzle.
- **Sandbox** — free mixing with every tube, no target.

### Where is my progress saved? Will I lose it?

Everything — your levels, stars, daily streak, paintings, custom levels, and settings — is stored **on your device**. There are no accounts, so progress doesn't sync between devices on its own. It is included in your encrypted iCloud/iTunes device backups if those are enabled, so restoring a device backup brings your progress with it.

### How does the Daily Challenge work?

There's one puzzle per day, the same for every player, generated from the date. Earn three stars to share a result card with friends.

### How do I create and share my own level?

Tap **Create a Level**, choose a target color and the tubes needed to mix it, then beat it once to confirm it's solvable. Tap **Share** to get a link — anyone with the app can open the link and play your level instantly. No account needed.

### How do I unlock the famous paintings in the Gallery?

You earn **florins** every time you beat a level (deeper levels pay more). Spend them in the **Gallery** to unlock famous public-domain masterpieces — each with a short, true story. Spending florins never lowers your rank. Add any you've unlocked to your **workshop wall**, where one shows behind the play area each level (rotating with your own paintings).

### How do I paint my own canvas?

Earn **three stars** on a level to unlock a blank canvas. Paint freely; your artwork is saved in your **Gallery** and framed on the next level's wall. You can share any painting from its detail view in the Gallery.

### I'm color-blind — can I still play?

Yes. Turn on **Color-blind mode** in Settings. It gives every tube a high-contrast tag — `R` / `Y` / `B` / `W` / `K` for the pure primaries, and a short unique code (like `OR`, `SL`, `MM`) for mixed colors, so no two tubes ever read the same. **Tap a tag to see that color's full name**, and the target shows its hex code (in Sandbox, the jar shows the live mixed color's name and hex as you pour). Every puzzle is solvable without relying on hue. Color Bar also supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, and Reduce Motion.

### How do I see a tutorial again?

Tap the **?** button during any level to replay that mechanic's walkthrough on demand. You can also turn the automatic interactive tutorials on or off in **Settings**.

### How do I reset my progress?

Deleting the app removes all on-device data (progress, paintings, custom levels, and settings). Reinstalling starts you fresh.
